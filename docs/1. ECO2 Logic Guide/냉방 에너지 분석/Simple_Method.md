## ğŸ”¹ Key Formula
The core equation for heat energy calculation is:

$$
Q = mc\Delta T
$$

Where:  
- **Q** = Heat energy (Joules)  
- **m** = Mass (kg)  
- **c** = Specific heat capacity (J/kgÂ·K)  
- **Î”T** = Temperature change (K)
